What is this service about?:

  • Exercise for rewards is a service that aims to encourage people to start their fitness journey and maintain their fitness by incentivising users through rewards that you can earn just by exercising!
  • Start your fitness journey by signing up and creating an account with our service!
  • Become eligible for rewards such as vouchers, free healthy snacks and more!.

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

Please have your address, contact details and fitness information ready to make the process as quick as possible. Exercise for Rewards requires this information in order to best recommend fitness options and offer support.

You can also register by post.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

You cannot register for this service if you are in the UK illegally.
